# KharchaX – Design Document

---

## 1. Visual Style & Mood

**Design Style:**
Modern, minimal, clean, and slightly playful (GenZ-friendly)

**Mood & Feel:**

* Smart
* Friendly
* Stress-free
* Trustworthy
* Slightly energetic

**Design Philosophy:**

* Keep UI simple → avoid clutter
* Focus on clarity of numbers and balances
* Make financial info easy to understand at a glance
* Use subtle animations for smooth experience

---

## 2. Color Palette

### Primary Colors:

* Primary: #6C5CE7 (Modern Purple – brand identity)
* Secondary: #00B894 (Fresh Green – success / balance)

### Accent Colors:

* Red (Debt): #FF4D4F
* Yellow (Warning): #FDCB6E
* Blue (Info): #0984E3

### Background Colors:

* Light Mode Background: #F8F9FB
* Dark Mode Background: #0F172A

### Text Colors:

* Primary Text: #1F2937
* Secondary Text: #6B7280
* White Text: #FFFFFF

---

## 3. Typography

### Font Family:

* Primary Font: Inter (clean and modern)
* Backup: system-ui, sans-serif

### Font Sizes:

* H1: 32px (bold)
* H2: 24px (semi-bold)
* H3: 18px (medium)
* Body: 14–16px (regular)
* Small text: 12px

### Font Weights:

* Bold: 700
* Medium: 500
* Regular: 400

---

## 4. Layout & Structure

### Layout Style:

* Centered layout
* Max width: 1200px
* Mobile-first responsive design

### Grid System:

* 12-column responsive grid

### Spacing:

* Padding: 16px / 24px
* Margin between sections: 24px–32px
* Card spacing: 16px

### Border Radius:

* Small: 8px
* Medium: 12px
* Large: 16px

---

## 5. UI Components

### Buttons:

* Rounded (10–12px radius)
* Primary: Purple background, white text
* Secondary: Outline style
* Hover: Slight brightness increase
* Active: Slight scale down (0.97)

---

### Cards:

* White background (light mode)
* Dark gray (dark mode)
* Soft shadow
* Rounded corners (12px)

Used for:

* Expense items
* Member cards
* Summary blocks

---

### Input Fields:

* Rounded corners
* Light border (#E5E7EB)
* Focus: purple border highlight

---

### Navigation Bar:

* Fixed top
* Clean minimal
* Logo on left (KharchaX)
* Menu on right

---

### Icons:

* Use simple line icons
* Style: minimal (Lucide / Heroicons style)

---

## 6. Page-Specific Design

---

### 6.1 Landing Page

**Hero Section:**

* Big bold heading:
  “Split smart. No drama.”
* Subtext explaining app
* CTA button: “Create Group”

**Sections:**

* Features grid (cards)
* How it works (3 steps)
* Benefits (AI + fairness)

---

### 6.2 Dashboard Page

**Top Section:**

* Total group expense
* Remaining balance

**Middle:**

* Cards:

  * “You owe”
  * “You get”

**Bottom:**

* Recent activity

---

### 6.3 Group Page

**Header:**

* Group name
* Members

**Main Section:**

* Expense list (cards):

  * Title
  * Amount
  * Paid by

**Right / Bottom:**

* Balance summary

---

### 6.4 Add Expense Page

* Clean form layout

* Fields:

  * Amount
  * Title
  * Paid by
  * Split among

---

### 6.5 AI Planner Page

**Input Section:**

* Number of people

* Suggested budget breakdown

### 6.6 Settlement Page

---

## 7. Animations & Interactions

* Smooth transitions (200–300ms)
* Card hover lift effect
* Button click feedback
* Subtle loading animations

---

## 8. Dark Mode

* Full support required
* Same layout, darker colors
* Maintain readability

---

## 9. Responsive Design

### Breakpoints:

* Mobile: < 640px
* Tablet: 640–1024px
* Desktop: > 1024px

### Behavior:

* Stack elements vertically on mobile
* Keep interactions touch-friendly

---

## 10. UX Principles

* Show important numbers clearly
* Minimize steps to add expense
* Avoid confusion in balances
* Use color to indicate:

  * Red = you owe
  * Green = you get

---

## 11. Design References

Inspiration from:

* Modern fintech apps
* Expense apps (clean dashboards)
* Minimal SaaS UI

Style direction:

* Clean like Notion
* Smooth like modern fintech apps
* Friendly like GenZ apps

---

## 12. Summary

KharchaX design should feel:

* Simple to use
* Smart and modern
* Friendly but not childish
* Focused on clarity and usability

The goal is to make expense management feel effortless and stress-free.
